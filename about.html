About Us

We are a sustainable chemistry solutions company distinguished by our innovative, science-driven products and expertise developed over decades. With operations across 4 continents, we manage 7 manufacturing facilities.

Our global team of 1,000+ employees is committed to advancing sustainable practices worldwide, with our deep-rooted values guiding our growth through prioritising customer success and delivering solutions that create lasting value for our clients.

The Solvent Chemicals Global Company owns large-scale, standardized fine chemical production facilities equipped with the largest organomolybdenum salt and thiazole compound production units with annual production volume exceeds 7,000 tons of functional chemicals.

The Solvent Chemicals Global Company remains committed to driving growth through innovation and aims to become a leader in the functional chemical industry. We work tirelessly to provide high-quality, efficient, and sustainable solutions for our global clientele.
